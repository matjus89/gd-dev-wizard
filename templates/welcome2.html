Grim Dawn devotion wizard

Enter the website now or take a quick look of how it works.

Blink mode lets you unlock any star with just one click. The app will reach the chosen star taking as little devotion points as possible while keeping to the in-game affinity requirements rules.
In order to activate the Blink Mode, click the 'Blink' button on the top of the page.

You can unlock stars and constellations this way as long as you have enough Devotion Points. You can also switch to Standard Mode if you wish to add or remove stars one by one.

Once the 'Standard' button is highlighted in green a click will only affect one star. All in-game Devotion Points and affinity requirements/bonuses still apply.

Results give you all the attributes which you've gained from unlocking the stars. You can easily navigate through them using arrows on the bottom of the results window.

You can reclaim all spent Devotion Points by clicking 'Reset' button. It works similarly to the in-game Tonic of Clarity.
